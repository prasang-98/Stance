Pay attention on posture of your students

Did you know posture affects student’s ability to learn, bad posture is considered as one of the major contributor of ADHD (Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder) and causes trouble focusing.
Increased sitting time, less physical activities, heavy school bags are few of the reasons why majority of students suffer from bad posture which can last for lifetime. As an educator (or parent) you should pay attention on posture of your students to improve their health and learning capabilities.
